Lasagna Recipe

Description

The is Mama Mia's world famous Lasagna recipe. This recipe created the now infamous cartoon Garfield.

Mama made this recipe with the specific intention to undermine America's democracy in order for her to usurp hegemony of the Northern Hemisphere.

Ingredients

  • Mama's famous secret special tomato sauce
  • Mozzerella and Ricotta from Mount Olympus
  • Lasagna pasta strips
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Make sauce with cooked ground beef
  2. Layer pasta strips, sauce, and cheese
  3. Bake until it looks yummy
